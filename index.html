01 About Me

Hi! I'm Jiayi Hu, an AI product enthusiast and data explorer from Lishui, Zhejiang, China.

I graduated from China University of Geosciences (Beijing) with a B.S. in Geographic Information Science. Currently, I'm pursuing a Master's in Surveying and Mapping Engineering (Spatiotemporal Big Data) at Wuhan University, admitted through the recommended exemption program. During my undergraduate studies, I maintained a GPA of 3.86 (top 5%), received the National Scholarship, and published an SCI Q2 paper as the first author.

I have extensive internship experience at companies including Baidu, Xiaohongshu (RED), and BaiRong Cloud. I specialize in transforming data insights into product strategies and am passionate about LLM applications, user growth, and data-driven product design. I also won two national-level awards in the China Undergraduate Mathematical Contest in Modeling.

My career goal is to become a Product Manager. If you'd like to learn more about my background and qualifications, feel free to download my resume.
